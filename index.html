  • Attendance Data: Clock-in/out times, break logs, overtime records, and location (optional).
  • Device Data: Device model, OS version, app version, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our attendance tracking services.

Delete Account

We're sorry to see you go. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including attendance logs, salary history, and profile information. This action is irreversible.

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the Self Attendance Pro app and go to Settings.
  • Scroll down and tap on Account.
  • Select Delete Account and confirm your password.
  • Tap Permanently Delete to finalize.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us with the subject "Delete Account Request" from your registered email address.

A confirmation email will be sent to verify your identity.
